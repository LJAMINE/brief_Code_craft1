Welcome to our awesome classroom where we will code the world! We are in a class called Codecraft, where we are learning the basics of coding. The main goal of this class is to introduce students to fundamental programming concepts through interactive and hands-on activities. We explore various topics by coding, solving problems, and collaborating on projects to strengthen our skills. The activities are diverse and designed to make learning to code engaging and fun while developing our critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.
